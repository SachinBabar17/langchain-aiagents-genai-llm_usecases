### 1. AI Customer Support Response Generator

Participants will build a customer support application where users enter a customer query along with optional information such as customer type, product category, and preferred response tone. The application first analyzes and summarizes the customer issue and then classifies it into categories such as Technical Issue, Billing Issue, Product Inquiry, Complaint, or General Query. Based on the identified category, a Branch Chain routes the request to a specialized prompt that generates an appropriate customer response. A final sequential step reviews and improves the generated response for clarity, professionalism, and tone consistency. The Streamlit interface can display the detected category, issue summary, generated response, and final refined response.

### 2. AI Resume and Job Description Analyzer

Participants will develop an application that accepts a candidate's resume text and a job description as input. The first step extracts important skills, experience, technologies, and qualifications from the resume. Another chain analyzes the job description to identify required skills and responsibilities. The outputs are then passed sequentially to a comparison chain that evaluates the candidate-job match, identifies missing skills, highlights strengths, and generates an overall suitability assessment. A final chain provides personalized recommendations for improving the resume or preparing for the role. The Streamlit UI can display the skill analysis, match assessment, missing skills, and improvement suggestions.

### 3. AI Content Repurposing Studio

Participants will create an application that transforms a single piece of content into different formats suitable for various communication platforms. The user enters a topic, source content, target audience, and preferred communication channel. A Sequential Chain first analyzes the content, extracts key ideas, and generates a concise content summary. A Branch Chain then routes the summarized content to specialized prompts based on the selected output format, such as a LinkedIn post, marketing email, blog article, or product description. A final refinement chain improves the generated content based on tone, readability, and audience requirements. The Streamlit application can allow users to compare the original content, summarized information, and final generated content.

### 4. AI Code Review Assistant

Participants will build an AI-powered code review application where users paste source code written in Python, Java, JavaScript, or another supported language. The first chain analyzes the submitted code and identifies potential issues related to bugs, performance, security, readability, or coding practices. A classification chain determines the primary type of issue detected. Based on the classification result, a Branch Chain routes the code to a specialized review prompt that generates detailed recommendations. A final Sequential Chain combines the analysis and recommendations into a structured code review report containing detected issues, explanations, suggested improvements, and an overall code quality assessment. Streamlit can provide a code input area and display the different stages of the review process.

### 5. AI Personalized Learning Path Generator

Participants will develop an application that creates customized learning plans based on a learner's background and goals. The user provides information such as current skills, experience level, learning objective, available study time, and preferred duration. A Sequential Chain first analyzes the learner profile and identifies skill gaps. A Branch Chain then routes the learner to a Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced learning-plan generator based on the identified proficiency level. The selected chain creates an appropriate learning roadmap containing topics, milestones, and practical activities. A final chain generates assessment questions or project recommendations to evaluate the learner's progress. The Streamlit UI can display the identified skill gaps, learner level, generated roadmap, and assessment activities.

### 6. AI Product Review Analyzer and Response Generator

Participants will create an application that processes customer product reviews and generates suitable responses. Users enter a product review along with optional product information. The first chain summarizes the review and extracts the main customer concerns or appreciation points. Another chain classifies the sentiment as Positive, Neutral, or Negative. A Branch Chain routes the review to specialized response-generation prompts based on the detected sentiment. Positive reviews receive appreciation responses, neutral reviews receive engagement-oriented responses, and negative reviews receive empathetic and resolution-focused responses. A final chain improves the generated response for professionalism and brand consistency. Streamlit can display the sentiment, review summary, important points, generated response, and final refined response.

### 7. AI Email Processing Assistant

Participants will build an application that analyzes incoming emails and generates appropriate responses. The user pastes an email into the Streamlit application and optionally selects the desired response tone. The first chain summarizes the email and extracts important information such as the sender's request, urgency, deadlines, and expected actions. A classification chain categorizes the email as Meeting Request, Technical Issue, Customer Complaint, Information Request, Follow-up, or General Communication. A Branch Chain routes the email to a specialized response-generation prompt based on the identified category. A final sequential step improves the response according to the selected tone and ensures that important information from the original email is addressed.

### 8. AI Smart Document Transformation Studio

Participants will build an application that transforms raw or unstructured text into different types of professional documents. The user can paste content such as meeting notes, technical notes, project updates, research notes, or rough ideas into the Streamlit interface and select the desired output type. A Sequential Chain first analyzes the input content, identifies the key information, extracts important points, and creates a structured summary. A Branch Chain then routes the processed content to specialized transformation chains based on the selected output type, such as **Meeting Minutes, Executive Summary, Technical Documentation, Project Status Report, or Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)**. A final Sequential Chain reviews the generated document for completeness, clarity, structure, and professional tone and produces the final polished output. The Streamlit application can display the extracted key information, selected document type, initial generated document, quality assessment, and final refined document. This project gives participants meaningful practice in prompt design, passing outputs between chains, conditional routing, managing multiple prompts, and building a complete LLM-powered application without requiring Agents or RAG.


### 9. AI Interview Preparation Coach

Participants will develop an interactive interview preparation application. The user enters a target job role, experience level, technology or skill area, and interview difficulty. A Sequential Chain first generates suitable interview questions based on the provided information. The user selects or answers a question through the Streamlit interface. Another chain evaluates the candidate's answer based on correctness, completeness, clarity, and technical depth. A Branch Chain routes the answer to different feedback generators based on the evaluation result, such as Strong Answer, Partially Correct Answer, or Weak Answer. A final chain generates an improved sample answer and personalized preparation recommendations. The application can display the evaluation score, strengths, improvement areas, and suggested answer.

### 10. AI Business Idea Validator

Participants will build an application that evaluates a proposed business idea and generates structured recommendations. The user provides information such as the business idea, target customers, problem being solved, proposed solution, and revenue model. A Sequential Chain first analyzes the business idea and identifies the value proposition, target market, strengths, risks, and assumptions. Another chain generates a viability assessment and classifies the idea as High Potential, Needs Refinement, or High Risk. A Branch Chain routes the idea to specialized recommendation prompts based on the classification result. High-potential ideas receive growth and execution recommendations, ideas requiring refinement receive improvement suggestions, and high-risk ideas receive pivot or validation recommendations. A final chain generates a concise action plan containing the next steps that the participant should take to validate or improve the business idea. The Streamlit UI can display the analysis, viability classification, recommendations, and final action plan.
